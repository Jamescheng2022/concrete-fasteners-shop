FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
For standard bolt and sleeve sizes, MOQ is 500 pcs. For anchor plates and custom fabricated items, MOQ is typically 50–100 pcs depending on complexity. Sample orders of 10–20 pcs are available for qualified buyers.
How long is the lead time for standard products?
Standard sizes are held in stock and can be dispatched within 3–5 business days. Custom dimensions or non-standard finishes typically require 10–15 business days from order confirmation.
Can you provide mill certificates and test reports?
Yes. Every shipment includes mill certificates for raw materials, dimensional inspection reports, and mechanical test results (tensile strength, hardness). Third-party inspection by SGS, BV or similar is welcome.
What surface treatment / coating options are available?
We offer: hot-dip galvanizing (HDG) to EN ISO 1461, electro-zinc plating, phosphate + oil, epoxy primer, and stainless steel (Grade 316). Custom coating specs for marine or aggressive environments available on request.
What shipping terms do you offer?
We quote FOB Qingdao/Shanghai/Tianjin as standard. CIF, DAP and DDU terms can be arranged. We work with established freight forwarders for FCL and LCL shipments worldwide.
Can you manufacture to our drawings or proprietary specifications?
Absolutely. Custom fabrication from DXF, DWG, PDF or STEP drawings is a core part of our business. OEM / private-label supply is also available for distributors with qualifying order volumes.
Are your lifting anchors certified to EN 13155?
Yes. Our lifting anchor range carries CE marking and is certified to EN 13155 (non-fixed load lifting attachments for concrete). Load test reports and FEM calculations are available for project documentation.
Do you accept payment by Letter of Credit (LC)?
Yes. We accept T/T (bank transfer), LC at sight, and Western Union for smaller orders. Payment terms vary by order size and trading history. Please discuss with our sales team.
