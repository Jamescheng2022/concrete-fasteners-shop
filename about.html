Our Story

Built on Engineering Integrity

ProConnect began as a small bolt machining operation in Shandong Province, China, supplying standard fasteners to local construction markets. From the outset, our founding engineers focused on dimensional accuracy and material traceability — values that remain central to our culture today.

As precast concrete construction expanded globally through the 2010s, we recognized the need for a dedicated supplier of structural connection components who understood both the engineering requirements and the production realities of precast factories. We invested in CNC turning centers, robotic welding, and a full in-house testing lab to serve this specialized market.

Today our 120,000 m² facility produces over 50 million connection components annually, exported to projects from residential towers in Singapore to infrastructure programs in the Middle East and transport networks across Europe.
